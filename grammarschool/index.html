This is my 4th grade class from Janko Veselinović grammar school in Belgrade, IV/1. Talking about highschool reunions, and picking up kids names brought back the old memories. I went to school with these little guys for 8 years, and still every name rings a bell. I wonder where they are today. I keep in touch with Boško & Rade.

If any of you find yourself through Google, this is Tota, the tiniest kid.
